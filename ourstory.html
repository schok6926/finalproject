    What is Open Door Opportunities (ODO) ?

    Our project compiles and spread resources, such as scholarships, free summer programs and college advice, to provide opportunities for all teenage students to succeed in the future. Education is key to social mobility and we hope to raise awareness of the opportunities that are available.



    Who created Open Door Opportunities (ODO) ?

    Four students at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program for their final project: Gianna, Ramya, Sara, and Sophia.



    Who came up with the idea and what was the source of inspiration for ODO?

    Gianna Bauzil, a student at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, came up with the idea for Open Door Opportunities. The inspiration for Open Door Opportunities came from her immigrant background and experience with scholarship and program applications. She realized that every teenager does not have the information or knowledge necessary in order to apply for financial and educational opportunities. She hopes that with the information on this website, all students, regardless of social or financial status, will be given the materials opportunities to excel in their chosen field.



    Why specifically focus on immigrants and women?

    Although our website aims to link high achieving students to opportunities, but we place a higher emphasis on promoting low-income immigrant and women to educational opportunities. Our website will include certain features, such as a community forum and women and immigrant spotlight pages to provide a sense of community and inspiration.

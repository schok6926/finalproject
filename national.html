    • Youth Forward Scholarship

    The Youth Forward Scholarship is open to current freshman high school and college students. You must submit an essay of no more than 500 words on your commitment to volunteerism in order to be considered for this award. This scholarship is available to all field studies. Learn more about the Youth Forward Scholarship on the DMV website.



    $20,000 Foot Locker Foundation Scholarship.

    Foot Locker will be awarding a $20,000 scholarship to incoming college freshman to help them obtain a 4-year college degree by demonstrating how sports have shaped them into a strong leader inside their school as well as their communities. One of the twenty chosen recipients of the scholarships, will have the opportunity to recieve the Ken C. Hicks Scholarship with an additional $5,000 for a total of $25,000. To find out more about the Foot Locker Scholarship check out their scholarship information on the provided link.



    National College Match Scholarship (QuestBridge)

    Recipients of the National College Match Scholarship will recieve admission into one of the QuestBridge college partners. Recipients are given a full four-year scholarship for over $200,000 each. This scholarship covers the cost of: tuition and fees, room and board, books and supplies, and travel expenses. Click here



    The Gates Scholarship

    The Gates Scholarship is highly selective scholarship among high school seniors. 300 top students are selected each year based on academic excellence, demonstrated leader ability, and personal success skills and give them the opportunity to atend a high end college.

    What is a College Budget?

    If college is one of your goal, having a plan to budget the cost will help you keep debt under control. College is expensive and knowing the different cost associated will help you manage it..

    Why does it matter?

    Costs can add up when attending college. Budgeting isn't something taught in school, but it can help you manage debt. Knowing what is spending is adding to your cost, you can manage your input and output.

    Tips & Tricks:

    1) Know your total debt beforehand. Every college in the US is required to have a college debt calculator that can give you an estimate of the tuition.

    2) There are additional costs besides tuition that you will need to account for, such as room & board and meal plan.

    3) Know how much financial aid and scholarship money you will be recieving so you can calculate and account for loans.

    4) Know the type of loads you will be getting, such as subsidized and unsubsidized loans.

    5) Start early! Budgeting early and slowly adding will keep the task from being overwhelming.

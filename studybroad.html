    CIEE High School Summer Abroad

    The Council of International Education Exchange, or CIEE, offers a variety of programs located in 50+ countries around the world, from Chile to South Korea. Programs include Language Study, Service & Leadership, and Global Discovery, where you may choose to concentrate in Arts, Environment, Business, or Politics. To assist in paying for the program, CIEE offers both Merit and Financial-based scholarships.

    National Security Language Initiative for Youth

    Part of a US government initiative to promote the learning of critical languages among American youth, the US State Department awards and administers full merit-based scholarships for high school students to study abroad and learn less commonly taught languages. Students may choose to apply to study one of the offered languages (Arabic, Mandarin, Hindi, Indonesian, Korean, Tajiki, Russian, Turkish) for either a summer or year abroad. There is also an option to study online through Virtual NSLI-Y.

    Kennedy-Lugar Youth Exchange & Study (YES Abroad)

    Like NSLI-Y, YES Abroad is also sponsored by the US State Department, offering full scholarships to study for one academic year in countries with significant Muslim populations. Serving as "youth ambassadors", participants live with a host family and attend a local high school, and will engage in activites that will enable them to learn about their host country and its values.

    Congress-Bundestag Youth Exchange for High School Students

    First initiated in 1983, the Congress-Bundestag Youth Exchange Program was created to strengthen diplomatic ties between Germany and the United States. CBYX participants spend an academic year living with a host family and attending a German high school, whilst gaining a new perspective on global current affairs and German social, economic, and political life. In addition, they will learn the German language through immersion at school and home.
